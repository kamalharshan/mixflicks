VAIRAMUTHU SONGS



Vairamuthu is a renowned Indian poet, lyricist, and author, primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born in 1953 in Tamil Nadu, he has written lyrics for over 7,500 songs, collaborating with leading composers like Ilaiyaraaja and A.R. Rahman. Vairamuthu's lyrics are celebrated for their poetic beauty, depth, and emotional resonance, often blending classical Tamil literature with contemporary themes. He has won numerous awards, including seven National Film Awards for Best Lyrics and the Padma Bhushan, India's third-highest civilian award. Vairamuthu is also a respected literary figure, with several acclaimed books and poems to his name.
