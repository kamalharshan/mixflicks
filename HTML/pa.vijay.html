PA VIJAY SONGS



Pa Vijay is a well-known Indian lyricist, poet, and filmmaker, primarily active in Tamil cinema. He gained recognition for his poignant and emotionally resonant lyrics, contributing to numerous hit songs since his debut in the late 1990s. Pa Vijay has collaborated with many leading composers, earning acclaim for his ability to capture a wide range of emotions in his lyrics. He won the National Film Award for Best Lyrics for the song "Ovvoru Pookalume" from Autograph (2004). Beyond lyric writing, Pa Vijay has also ventured into acting and filmmaking, showcasing his versatility in the Tamil entertainment industry.
