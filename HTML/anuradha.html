ANURADHA SONGS



Anuradha Sriram is a versatile Indian playback singer known for her work in Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Kannada, and Hindi cinema. Born in 1970 in Chennai, she is classically trained in Carnatic and Hindustani music, which adds depth to her performances across various genres. Anuradha made her playback debut in the mid-1990s and quickly became popular for her energetic and expressive singing style. She has sung in numerous hit films and is also recognized for her work in devotional music. Apart from singing, Anuradha is an accomplished television host and stage performer, admired for her dynamic presence and wide-ranging talent.
