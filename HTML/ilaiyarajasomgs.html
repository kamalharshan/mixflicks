ILAIYARAJA SONGS



Ilaiyaraaja is a legendary Indian composer, conductor, and songwriter, hailed as one of the greatest music composers in Indian cinema. Born in 1943 in Pannaipuram, Tamil Nadu, he has composed over 7,000 songs and scores for more than 1,000 films, predominantly in Tamil, Telugu, and Malayalam cinema. Known as the "Isaignani" (The Musical Genius), Ilaiyaraaja is renowned for his innovative fusion of Western classical music with Indian traditional sounds. He was the first Asian to compose a full symphony for the Royal Philharmonic Orchestra. His profound influence on Indian music is both vast and enduring.
