SWARNALATHA SONGS



Swarnalatha was a gifted Indian playback singer known for her unique voice and emotive singing style. Born in 1973 in Tamil Nadu, she made her debut in 1987 with the song "Chinnachiru Kiliye" composed by Ilaiyaraaja. Swarnalatha sang in several Indian languages, including Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, and Hindi, and was renowned for her versatility and ability to convey deep emotion. Her song "Poraale Ponnuthayi" from Karuthamma earned her a National Film Award in 1995. Swarnalatha's voice was celebrated for its richness and adaptability, making her a beloved figure in Indian cinema until her untimely death in 2010.
