MANI RATNAM MOVIES





Mani Ratnam, born on June 2, 1956, is a legendary Indian filmmaker known for revolutionizing Tamil and Indian cinema with his unique storytelling and visual style. His directorial debut was Pallavi Anu Pallavi (1983), but he gained fame with Mouna Ragam and Nayakan. Ratnam is celebrated for films like Roja, Bombay, and Dil Se, blending complex narratives with strong emotional cores. His work often addresses social and political themes, and he is known for collaborating with composer A.R. Rahman. Mani Ratnam's films have won numerous national and international awards, cementing his legacy in Indian cinema.
