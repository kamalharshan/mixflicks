RAJINI MOVIES





J Rajinikanth, born Shivaji Rao Gaekwad on December 12, 1950, is a legendary Indian actor and cultural icon. Known for his unique style and charismatic screen presence, he has dominated Tamil cinema for over four decades.Rajinikanth's journey from a bus conductor to a superstar is inspirational. His roles often portray the common man, and his films blend action, comedy, and drama.With blockbuster hits like Baashha, Enthiran, and Kabali, he's become one of Asia's highest-paid actors. Beyond cinema, Rajinikanth enjoys a massive fan following, revered almost as a deity by many in South India.
