VAALI SONGS



Vaali, born T. S. Rangarajan in 1931, was a legendary Indian poet and lyricist, known for his prolific contribution to Tamil cinema. Over a career spanning five decades, he penned lyrics for over 10,000 songs, working with multiple generations of composers from M.S. Viswanathan to A.R. Rahman. Vaali's lyrics are admired for their versatility, wit, and depth, seamlessly blending classical Tamil poetry with modern themes. He also wrote dialogues, screenplays, and acted in several films. Vaali received numerous awards, including the Padma Shri and multiple Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, leaving an indelible mark on Tamil cinema and literature.
