ANIRUDH SONGS



Anirudh Ravichander is a contemporary Indian music composer and singer, primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born in 1990 in Chennai, he burst onto the music scene with his debut song "Why This Kolaveri Di" from the film 3 (2012), which became a viral sensation. Anirudh's music is characterized by catchy tunes, energetic beats, and innovative production, blending various genres like EDM, pop, and Indian classical. He has quickly become one of the most sought-after composers in South Indian cinema, with numerous chart-topping hits and a growing fanbase across India and beyond.
