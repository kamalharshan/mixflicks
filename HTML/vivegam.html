VIVEGAM






Vivegam is a 2017 Indian Tamil-language military science fiction action film directed by Siva and produced by Sathya Jyothi Films. It stars Ajith Kumar in the lead role, alongside Vivek Oberoi, Kajal Aggarwal, Akshara Haasan, Karunakaran, Aarav Chowdhary, Bharath Reddy and Sharat Saxena. In the film, A celebrated covert operation specialist Ajay Kumar (AK) is assigned a high risk mission to retrieve the detonation codes for deadly plutonium weapons stolen by terrorists to upset the world order. After putting his unparalleled skills to use he finds himself on the verge of successfully completing the mission, only to be shocked by treasonous acts which he wouldn't have even dreamt of. He is then left to deal with overwhelming odds stacked against him in order to survive.
