DHANUSH SONGS



Dhanush,born Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja on July 28, 1983, is a prominent Indian actor, filmmaker, and playback singer known primarily for his work in Tamil cinema. He made his acting debut with Thulluvadho Ilamai (2002) and gained widespread acclaim with Kadhal Kondein (2003). Dhanush's versatile performances in films like Aadukalam, Velaiilla Pattadhari, and Asuran have won him several awards, including National Film Awards.
