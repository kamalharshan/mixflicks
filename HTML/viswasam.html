VISWASAM






Viswasam is a 2019 Indian Tamil-language action drama film[5][6] directed by Siva, who also co-wrote the screenplay. The film stars Ajith Kumar, Nayanthara and Jagapathi Babu. Produced by Sathya Jyothi Films, the film was officially announced on 20 November 2017 and principal photography began in May 2018.
