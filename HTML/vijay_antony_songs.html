VIJAY ANTONY SONGS



Vijay Antony is an Indian music composer, playback singer, actor, and film producer known for his contributions to Tamil cinema. Born in 1975, he began his career as a music composer, gaining fame with his debut soundtrack for Sukran (2005). Vijay Antony's music, often featuring catchy tunes and electronic influences, became popular, with hits like "Naaka Mukka" bringing him widespread recognition. He transitioned to acting with his debut film Naan (2012), which was both a commercial and critical success. Vijay Antony is known for his unique choice of roles and his ability to balance his music and acting careers.
