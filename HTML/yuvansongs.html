YUVAN SHANKAR RAJA SONGS



Yuvan Shankar Raja is a prominent Indian music composer and singer known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born in 1979, he is the son of legendary composer Ilaiyaraaja. Yuvan debuted in 1996 with the film Aravindhan and gained widespread recognition for his fresh, modern sound that blends electronic music, hip-hop, and traditional Indian melodies. He is celebrated for his soulful melodies and innovative background scores. Yuvan became the youngest composer to win a Filmfare Award for Best Music Director in 2004. With numerous hits, he is a trendsetter in Tamil music, influencing a new generation of composers.
