VIVEK SONGS



Vivek is a contemporary Indian lyricist known for his work in Tamil cinema. He gained prominence for his collaboration with composer Anirudh Ravichander, contributing to several chart-topping songs. Vivek's lyrics are appreciated for their modern sensibility, catchy phrases, and the ability to connect with younger audiences. Some of his popular works include songs from films like Vivegam ("Surviva"), Mersal ("Aalaporaan Thamizhan"), and Master ("Kutty Story"). He has worked with leading composers and directors, earning a reputation for his versatility and knack for creating memorable, impactful lyrics that resonate with a wide audience.
