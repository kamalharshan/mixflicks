K S RAVIKUMAR MOVIES





K. S. Ravikumar is a highly regarded Indian film director, screenwriter, and actor known for his work in Tamil cinema. Born on May 30, 1958, he has directed over 40 films, many of which became blockbusters. Ravikumar is celebrated for his versatility, seamlessly blending comedy, action, and drama in his films. His notable works include Muthu (1995), Padayappa (1999), Dasavathaaram (2008), and Lingaa (2014). Ravikumar has worked with top actors like Rajinikanth and Kamal Haasan and is known for his efficient, fast-paced direction. His contributions have made him a respected figure in Indian cinema.
