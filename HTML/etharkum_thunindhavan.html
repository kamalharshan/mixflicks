ETHARKKUM THUNINDHAVAN






Etharkkum Thunindhavan , also known by the initialism ET, is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film,[1] written and directed by Pandiraj, and produced by Sun Pictures. The film stars Suriya, Vinay Rai and Priyanka Mohan. D. Imman composed the film's music, R. Rathnavelu handled the cinematography and Ruben edited it. The film revolves around a lawyer who opposes a pornography racket that is run by a prominent minister's son.
