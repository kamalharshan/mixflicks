CHITHRA SONGS



K.S. Chithra, often referred to as the "Nightingale of South India," is a highly celebrated Indian playback singer. Born in 1963 in Kerala, she has an illustrious career spanning over four decades, singing in various Indian languages including Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and Hindi. Chithra's voice is renowned for its clarity, emotional depth, and versatility, making her a favorite across genres. She has won six National Film Awards, eight Filmfare Awards South, and numerous other accolades. With over 25,000 songs to her credit, Chithra is a revered figure in Indian music, admired for her contributions to both film and devotional music.
